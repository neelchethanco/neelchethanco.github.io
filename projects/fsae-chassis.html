Overview

As part of Mason Motorsports' FSAE chassis sub-team, I contributed to the structural design and analysis of the team's steel spaceframe chassis. The goal was to maximize torsional rigidity and minimize weight while meeting all SAE International structural requirements for driver safety.

Design Requirements

  • Comply with FSAE 2024 rulebook — main roll hoop, front hoop, and side impact structure geometry
  • Target torsional stiffness ≥ [X] N·m/deg to match suspension design targets
  • Minimize unsprung mass contribution without compromising rigidity
  • Design for in-house TIG welding and tube bending capability

Structural Analysis

Finite element analysis was performed in ANSYS to evaluate chassis behavior under torsional, bending, and impact load cases. Critical nodes were identified at suspension pickup points and roll hoop junctions. Tube wall thickness and triangulation geometry were iterated to eliminate stress concentrations.
