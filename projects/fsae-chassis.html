FSAE Steel Spaceframe
Chassis Program

Team  Phoenix Motorsports Role  President & Lead Engineer Span  Sep 2023 – Jun 2025

Program Overview

As co-founder and President of Phoenix Motorsports, I led the full chassis development program from a blank sheet to a welded steel spaceframe. Rather than jumping straight to fabrication, the program followed a deliberate three-stage process: two full-scale PVC pipe mockups to validate and refine geometry at low cost, followed by the final steel build. Each stage fed directly into the next, reducing risk and improving the final design.
